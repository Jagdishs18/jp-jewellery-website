Refund & Exchange Policy

Customer Satisfaction is Our Priority

✨ Lifetime Exchange on Gold Jewellery

JP Jewellery offers lifetime exchange on all gold jewellery purchased from our store. You can exchange your gold pieces for new designs anytime, paying only the difference in weight and making charges plus applicable taxes.

Exchange Terms

  • Original invoice must be presented at the time of exchange
  • Jewellery must be in original condition (except normal wear and tear)
  • Exchange value is based on current day's gold rate
  • Making charges and taxes are applicable on the new piece
  • Diamond and gemstone jewellery can be exchanged within 30 days of purchase

Buyback Policy

We offer buyback of gold jewellery at prevailing market rates. The buyback value is calculated as:

  • Gold value = (Weight in grams × Current gold rate) minus applicable charges
  • Diamond value is assessed based on current market rates and certification
  • Making charges are not refunded in buyback transactions

Refund Policy

For online purchases, refunds are processed under the following conditions:

  • Damaged/Defective Products: Full refund within 7 days of delivery
  • Wrong Product Delivered: Full refund or replacement at no cost
  • Change of Mind: Not applicable for jewellery due to hygiene and security reasons

How to Initiate Exchange or Refund

For in-store purchases, visit our Tirupati store with the original invoice. For online orders, contact our customer service at 092469 99951 or email info@jpjewellery.in within the return window.

Exclusions

Custom-made jewellery, engraved pieces, and products with missing certificates cannot be returned or exchanged. Polki, Kundan, and antique finish pieces are subject to special evaluation.
