Cancellation & Return Policy

Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement without any penalty. To cancel, please contact our concierge at +91 99344 34485 or email concierge@sonachandi.com.

Once production has begun on custom pieces, cancellations cannot be accepted.

Return Policy

We accept returns within 15 days of delivery for eligible items. Items must be unused, unworn, and in original packaging with all certificates and tags attached.

How to Initiate a Return

  • Contact our concierge team for a Return Authorization Number (RAN)
  • Pack the item securely in original packaging
  • Ship using insured shipping method
  • Once received and inspected, refund will be processed

Exchange Policy

Exchanges are available for size adjustments or alternative designs within 30 days of purchase, subject to availability.
